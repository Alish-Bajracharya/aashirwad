About Accommodation

Nestled in the heart of Pokhara’s scenic Lakeside, our hotel offers the perfect blend of comfort and Himalayan charm. Just a short stroll from the tranquil waters of Phewa Lake, guests can enjoy breathtaking mountain views right from their rooms.

Each space is designed with warm wooden accents, modern amenities, and large windows to welcome in the natural light. Whether you are here for adventure or relaxation, you’ll find our service attentive, the ambiance peaceful, and the location unbeatable.

After a day of exploring the Annapurna foothills or boating on the lake, return to your sanctuary where comfort meets elegance. Indulge in fresh local cuisine, unwind at our rooftop lounge, and let the serenity of Pokhara complete your stay.
